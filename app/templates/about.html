MeBots is an toolkit for building, sharing, and using GroupMe chat bots with ease. It's a public list of bots similar to GroupMe's bot system MeBots extends GroupMe's bot system and adds new features, such as a user interface for adding bots to groups, abstracted storage of basic bot information, and more!

The MeBots API makes bot development way easier. It's ideal for simplifying development and maintenance of bots at many scales, as well as for promoting them through the Bot Shop. Anyone can sign in with GroupMe, browse through your bots, and add what they like to servers they're in.

What are you waiting for? Create your first bot and dive into the documentation!
