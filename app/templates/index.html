MeBots is an API toolkit for running GroupMe chat bots with ease. MeBots provides tools GroupMe's native bot system doesn't, such as a ready-made interface for adding bots to different servers, hosted storage of bot instance information, and wrapping around other less convenient elements of the GroupMe bots API.

The MeBots API is ideal for simplifying development and maintenance of bots at many scales, as well as for promoting them to the through the Bot Shop. Anyone can sign in with GroupMe, browse through your bots, and add what they like to servers they're in.

What are you waiting for? Create your first bot and dive into the documentation!
